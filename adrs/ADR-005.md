# ADR-005

# Title

Resume Before Search

---

## Status

Accepted

---

## Context

Most knowledge tools optimize for searching.

Kisuke optimizes for continuing interrupted work.

---

## Decision

Resume is the primary workflow.

Search exists only to support resume.

Every major feature must improve context reconstruction before improving search.

---

## Consequences

### Positive

- Lower cognitive load
- Faster work continuation
- Clear product identity

### Negative

- More complex resume engine

---

## Alternatives Considered

Search-first.

Rejected.

Reason:

Does not solve the primary problem Kisuke exists to solve.
