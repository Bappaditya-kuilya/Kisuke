# ADR-003

# Title

Provider Independent AI

---

## Status

Accepted

---

## Context

AI providers evolve rapidly.

Kisuke must never depend on a single provider.

---

## Decision

All AI communication goes through a Provider Interface.

Supported providers include:

- OpenAI
- Anthropic
- Gemini
- Ollama
- OpenRouter
- OpenAI-compatible APIs

The Core never knows which provider is being used.

---

## Consequences

### Positive

- No vendor lock-in
- Easy provider switching
- Local or cloud support
- Future proof

### Negative

- Additional abstraction layer

---

## Alternatives Considered

Direct provider SDKs.

Rejected.

Reason:

Vendor lock-in.
